meVBT
Device-grade rep velocity — and smarter load & RPE calls — from the gear you already own.
Visual brief · May 2026

Every velocity app is chasing the same useful thing: turn how fast the bar moves into smarter training — gauge how hard a set was and what to load next. Today that means buying a $300 bar device, or filming a perfectly side-on video of perfectly round plates. meVBT does it from the Apple Watch & AirPods already on your body — and when you do film, it just works: off-angle, odd plates, low frame rate.

01 The job to be done — turn rep speed into the next call

Bar speed on the last hard rep tells you how close to failure you were — and whether to add, hold, or drop weight. Learn each lifter’s personal curve and it becomes a real-time RPE gauge.

03 One velocity model, whatever sensor you have

Watch, AirPods, phone video, or a bar device — each emits the same shape (rep boundaries · velocity · ROM · confidence) into one model. Use whatever’s present; lean on a personal prior when the signal is thin. And it’s not tied to one brand: the same model runs off an Apple Watch, a Fitbit, a Garmin, a Whoop — even a generic IMU you tape on — so it works with the gear you already own.
